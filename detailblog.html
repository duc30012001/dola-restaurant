Tell you how to make delicious and flavorful fish soup

Monday, 24/10/2022
Admin Dola

Cooked fish soup is a rustic dish, familiar in every Vietnamese family meal. In the hot summer, there is nothing better than having a bowl of sweet and sour fish soup. There are many ways to cook according to this recipe such as: batch cooked carp soup, batch cooked basa fish soup, batch cooked snakehead fish soup, batch cooked fish along the net soup, etc.

So what ingredients do you need to prepare?, How to cook fish soup cooked in batches to have the most delicious and delicious soup. Please follow the article below with Dola

The recipe for making fish soup cooked in batches the whole family will love

In order to make a delicious and nutritious soup, the selection of ingredients is essential, so you need to choose carefully. The ingredients to cook this soup are very simple and easy to find.

Ingredients to be prepared

  • Fresh fish.
  • Tomato.
  • Batch rice.
  • Turmeric.
  • Red onion, green onion, dill, Chinese smell, chili.
  • Some necessary spices for the dish: salt, seasoning, fish sauce, ground pepper, …

Preliminary processing of cooked fish ingredients

  • Fresh fish: You should choose to buy live fish to ensure freshness, moderate size, firm flesh is delicious fish. Buy fish, scale it, wash it, then cut open the belly to remove all the fish intestines. Chop the fish into bite-sized pieces, then marinate a little seasoning with salt, fish sauce, and seasoning for 30 minutes.
  • Tomatoes: Should choose to buy tomatoes with glossy skin, pinkish red color, round shape, fully ripe to make the dish more delicious. Wash tomatoes, add the shape of areca pods.
  • Green onions, dill, smell of Chinese picked up, washed, cut into pieces about 2cm.
  • Peel turmeric, pound the juice, remove the residue.
  • Shredded shallots, chopped or pureed with a spice grinder.
  • Chilli washed, cut into small pieces.
  • Batch rice is filtered through a sieve to get 1 bowl of cold water.

 

 

Steps to prepare delicious fish soup with the right taste

The cooking part is also a very important part, determining the success of the dish. So, let's refer to the steps to prepare batch cooked fish soup below:

  • Step 1: Put the pan on the stove, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil, heat the cooking oil, then put the fish in and fry it, take it out and drain the oil..
  • Step 2: Put the pot on the stove, saute the purple onion, add a small amount of water to taste, and then taste the seasoning. Next, add tomatoes, cold water, turmeric juice and bring to a boil. Next, add the fish and bring to a boil over low heat until cooked.
  • Step 3: Add scallions, dill, coriander, taste again and then turn off the stove. Ladle into bowls and enjoy.

So with just 3 basic steps above, you have a cool and cool fish soup for the whole family. If you are wondering what to eat today then try this dish right away..
